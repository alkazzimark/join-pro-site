Overview

Join Pro is a free Autodesk Revit add-in that automates priority-based joining of structural elements (walls, columns, structural framing, floors, roofs, and structural foundations) across Revit 2021–2026.

Features

  • Priority-based joining with a simple reorderable list.
  • Supports walls, columns, structural framing, floors, roofs, and structural foundations.
  • Batch joining, unjoin all, and disallow beam end joins.
